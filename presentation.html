Bar and Horizontal Bar

Use these when you want the clearest comparison across options. Horizontal mode works especially well for long survey answers.

Pie and Doughnut

Use these when the question is about share of total and the number of answer options is small enough to stay readable.

Radar and Polar Area

Use these when you want the chart to feel more presentation-forward and highlight variation between response groups.

Line

Use this when the responses follow an order, such as numeric bands or progression from one category to the next.
